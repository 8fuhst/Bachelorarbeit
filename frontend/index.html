Keystroke Data Collection

Hey, and thank you for participating in my study! On the next page, you will be asked to type some randomly selected words in a textfield. Once you've completed a word, the next one will automatically appear. You can type as many words as you like, there is no finish line, data is saved after every word. If you make a typo, just correct it and finish the word normally. Do mind that the same word can appear two times in a row - once the textfield is cleared, you're good to go!

Please note, that the intention of this study is to collect data via keyboards. Thus, please refrain from using this website with a mobile, tablet, or other touch based input devices!
